Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 17, 2025

Introduction

DailyBudget ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for daily budget tracking and expense management.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Financial Data:

DailyBudget processes your expense and budget data locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your financial information to our servers or any third-party services. All budget tracking and expense processing happens entirely on your device.

Budget and Expense Data:

Your budget information, including expenses, spending categories, and financial statistics are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

Usage Data:

We do not collect analytics data or usage statistics. DailyBudget operates entirely offline on your device.

Local Storage:

The app stores your preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Budget database and expense records
  • App settings and preferences
  • Spending history and categories
  • Financial goals and savings targets
  • Budget alerts and notifications

How We Use Your Information

We use locally stored information to:

  • Provide daily budget tracking services
  • Maintain your personal expense database
  • Track spending amounts and categories
  • Calculate budget limits and savings
  • Provide financial statistics and insights
  • Remember your app preferences and settings

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your financial data and budget information remain on your device.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Data Security

Your financial data and budget information remain on your device. We do not transmit or store any financial data externally. While we take steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

Your budget data and app preferences are stored locally and remain there until you delete the app or reset your settings. We do not retain any personal data or financial information on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Third-Party Services

DailyBudget does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting services, or cloud storage services. All processing is done locally on your device.

Budget Tracking Technology

Our budget tracking technology uses on-device calculations and data storage to manage your expenses and budgets. No financial information is sent to external servers for processing.

App Store Compliance

DailyBudget complies with both Apple's App Store and Google Play Store guidelines and privacy requirements:

  • No tracking or analytics collection
  • No data sharing with third parties
  • Local-only data processing
  • Transparent privacy practices
  • Cross-platform consistency

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the app)
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns
  • Request information about our data practices

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@dailybudget.app
  • Website: https://dailybudget.app
